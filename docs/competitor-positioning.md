# Competitor Positioning

Well and Good Websites should not be positioned around the sales tactic of showing a draft in person. That can remain part of the sales process, but the public promise should be simpler:

> Affordable, custom one-page websites for local businesses, live in 48 hours, designed to help customers call, visit, book, or request a quote.

## What Owners Compare Against

| Alternative | What they believe | Weakness Well and Good can own |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Local agencies | Professional, but expensive and slow | Affordable fixed packages, fast turnaround, no long discovery process |
| Freelancers | Cheaper than agencies | Clear scope, local focus, practical conversion structure |
| DIY builders | Low monthly cost, self-serve control | Done-for-you custom site without setup friction or blank-page work |
| Booking pages | Already handles appointments | Booking pages are checkouts, not owned storefronts or local trust pages |
| Social profiles only | Easy to update, already active | Social is not a clear home base for Google searches, referrals, hours, services, and calls |
| No website | Good enough if word of mouth works | Referred customers still check online before calling, visiting, booking, or requesting a quote |
| Friend or family build | Feels cheap and familiar | Unclear timeline; Well and Good is fixed-scope and live in 48 hours after approval |

## Positioning Pillars

- **Affordable:** priced for owner-operated local businesses, not agency budgets.
- **Custom:** built around the actual business, services, reviews, location, and customer actions.
- **Fast:** live in 48 hours after approved details and assets.
- **Action-focused:** designed around calls, visits, bookings, and quote requests.
- **Local:** practical for Niagara and Toronto area businesses that need a clear web presence without overbuying.

## Avoid

- Do not lead with "tablet-first" or "in-person walkthrough" as the brand position.
- Do not call the work "cheap" in public copy; use affordable, fixed-price, or priced for local businesses.
- Do not sound like a full-service agency selling strategy, branding, SEO retainers, or complex funnels.
- Do not overclaim search rankings or guaranteed revenue.

## Useful Copy

- Affordable custom websites, live in 48 hours.
- A real website for your business, without the agency price or long wait.
- Built around the actions your customers need most: call, visit, book, or request a quote.
- Custom to your business, not a generic template.
- Simple one-page websites for local businesses that need a clear online home base.
