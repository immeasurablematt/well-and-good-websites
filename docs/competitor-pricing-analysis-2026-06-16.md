## 0. The Strategy in One Paragraph

Simple website-building agencies sell a one-time build and stop. You can sell the build
**plus** the growth services that actually make a local business money — SEO, Google
Business Profile, social, and content — backed by **10+ years of real results** (see §6).
Because you produce and maintain everything with AI at near-zero marginal cost, you can
**bundle services that competitors sell separately for $2,000–$4,000/month, price the
bundle below a single competitor's one service, publish the price openly (no one else
does), and still keep ~95% margins.** This turns you from a website vendor into a local
growth partner — a category the website-only shops can't follow you into without your
expertise, and the growth agencies can't match on price.

## 2. Competitor Landscape — Growth Services (the key finding)

**5 of 8 local/affordable competitors already bundle growth services** (SEO, social,
and/or paid media): Cool Koala, Symetric, KMK, Niagara Web Design, MIM. Vision and
CanadaWebPro bundle SEO only; Tenpine does SEO + "digital marketing."

**But not one of the eight publishes a single price for any growth service.** Every SEO,
social, and paid-media offering is "free consultation / request a quote." Website pricing
is occasionally shown as a range; **growth-service pricing is universally hidden.** That is
the gap: a published, productized growth offer is something no competitor in this set has.

### What a customer pays competitors for the full stack
A local business buying SEO **and** social separately from the mid-market typically pays
**~$1,800–$5,500/month** ($800–$2,500 SEO + $1,000–$3,000 social), plus a one-time
build of **$2,500–$8,000** — and gets opaque, quote-gated pricing. **That combined
$1,800–$5,500/month is the number your bundle undercuts.**

## 4. Your Real Costs (the moat, quantified)

| Cost item | Per build | Per month / client |
|---|---|---|
| Static hosting (Cloudflare Pages free, commercial-OK) | $0 | $0 |
| Domain (.ca ~$11–$33/yr) | — | ~$1–$3 (client usually owns) |
| AI tokens — build, copy, SEO content, social posts, reports | a few dollars | low single-digit dollars |
| Your labor (strategy, prompting, QA, client comms) | a few hours | a few hours/month, falling as you systematize |

Direct COGS is effectively **$0–$5/client/month** plus token spend. The binding constraint
is **your time**, which AI compresses dramatically: drafting a month of social posts, a
location/service page, or a performance report is minutes, not days. **Gross margin at the
prices proposed below is ~90–97%** even at the heaviest tier. Undercutting the market by
60–80% costs you almost nothing.

## 5. Proposed Tier Architecture

Three things sold together, so each flagship tier carries **both** a one-time price (build +
onboarding) and a recurring price (ongoing growth). Standalone and à-la-carte options exist
for flexibility, but the **flagship Website + Growth plans are the hero offer** — they're
what make you uncatchable by website-only shops.

### A. Flagship plans — Website + Growth (one-time + recurring)

#### 🟢 LAUNCH — get found locally
- **One-time: $497** (build + setup) · **Recurring: $99/mo** (or $990/yr — 2 months free)
- **One-time includes:** Starter one-page website (mobile, tap-to-call, hours/map/reviews),
  Google Business Profile setup & optimization, on-page SEO foundation, core local
  citations, one social profile set up and branded.
- **Recurring includes:** commercial hosting + SSL + backups + monitoring, monthly content
  refresh / small edits, ongoing GBP management (posts, hours, photos), **4 social posts/mo
  on 1 platform**, review-request prompts, simple monthly results email.
- **Best for:** barbers, salons, trades, cafés who just need to show up and get calls.
- **Undercuts:** a basic local-SEO retainer alone ($285–$500/mo) — and you include the site,
  hosting, GBP, and social on top.

#### 🔵 GROW — more calls, bookings & quotes *(most popular)*
- **One-time: $997** (build + onboarding) · **Recurring: $399/mo** (or $3,990/yr)
- **One-time includes:** Standard website (gallery, booking/quote flow), keyword research &
  local SEO strategy, GBP optimization, citation building, competitor scan, social profiles
  set up (2 platforms), content calendar, review-funnel page.
- **Recurring includes:** everything in Launch, plus —
  - **Active local SEO:** ongoing on-page optimization + **2 location/service or blog pages
    per month**, citation upkeep, technical health checks.
  - **Managed social: 2 platforms, ~12 posts/month** (graphics + captions + hashtags),
    community engagement, light short-form video/Reel monthly.
  - Review management (request flow + responses), bi-weekly optimization, **monthly
    performance report with calls/clicks/bookings + plain-English next steps.**
- **Best for:** owner-operated businesses serious about growth.
- **Undercuts:** buying SEO ($800–$1,500) + social ($950–$1,500) separately ≈ $1,800–$3,000/mo
  → you deliver both **plus** the site and care for **$399/mo (~80% less).**

#### 🟣 DOMINATE — own your local market
- **One-time: $1,497** (premium build + full onboarding) · **Recurring: $899/mo** (or $8,990/yr)
- **One-time includes:** Premium website (custom copy, before/after or menu, review funnel),

- **Recurring includes:** everything in Grow, plus —
  - **Aggressive SEO:** broader keyword targets, **4 content pieces/month** (location +

  - **Managed social: 3–4 platforms, ~20 posts/month** incl. **weekly short-form video/Reels**,
    full content production, active community management.
  - **Monthly strategy call**, advanced reporting dashboard, priority turnaround, seasonal

- **Undercuts:** comparable agency SEO+social+content bundles run **$2,500–$5,000+/mo** — you

## 7. Positioning — How You Win

- **Be the only one with published prices.** Every competitor hides SEO/social pricing;
  71% of businesses prefer transparent pricing and 63% have been burned by hidden fees.
  A clear price card is a differentiator *and* an SEO asset (targets "website design toronto
  prices", "social media management cost", "local seo pricing niagara").
- **Sell outcomes, name the moat lightly:** "Modern AI tooling + 10 years of experience, so
  you get agency-grade SEO, social, and a custom site for a fraction of agency prices."
- **Lead with the bundle math:** "What agencies charge for SEO *or* social alone, you get
  both — plus your website — for less."
- **No lock-in** (monthly cancel anytime; annual prepaid). Undercut the 6–12 month contracts
  competitors require.
- **Land-and-expand:** win the cheap build, upgrade into Launch (first month free), then Grow.

## 9. Open Decisions for the Owner
- **Bundle vs. standalone emphasis:** recommendation is to lead with the flagship Website +
  Growth plans and keep build-only/à-la-carte as fallbacks. Confirm you're comfortable
  leading with recurring.
- **Recurring price points:** $99 / $399 / $899 is the recommendation (still 60–80% under
  market). A more aggressive land-grab set would be $79 / $349 / $799; a higher-ARPU set
  $129 / $499 / $999.
- **Social platform/post volumes** per tier (4 / 12 / 20 posts proposed) — adjust to the time
  you want to commit; these define your labor cap.
- **Content cadence** (0 / 2 / 4 pieces per month) — same consideration.
- **Paid ads fee:** 12% + $250 min recommended; confirm you want to manage ad budgets at all.
- **Capacity:** at full service these tiers are time-bound by you — decide a max client count
  per tier before opening sales, and lean on AI/templates to raise that ceiling.
- Billing tool (Stripe subscriptions recommended); whether to require annual on the top tier.
